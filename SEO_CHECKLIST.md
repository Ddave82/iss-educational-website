- Add `https://iss-education.online` as a property in Google Search Console.
- Submit `https://iss-education.online/sitemap.xml`.
- Inspect these URLs in Search Console and request indexing after launch:
  - `https://iss-education.online/`
  - `https://iss-education.online/tracker`

  - `https://iss-education.online/see-the-iss`

- Confirm the Vercel domain redirects consistently to `https://iss-education.online`.
- Monitor indexed pages, crawl errors, search queries, and Core Web Vitals in Search Console.
- Re-submit the sitemap after major content or route changes.
